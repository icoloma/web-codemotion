Why volunteer at Codemotion?

Codemotion is organized by a handful of volunteers, and when the day comes there should be a lot of us to assume all that is coming:

  • Help in the rooms before, after and during the talks.
  • Prepare the aisles and stands for the two days.
  • Prepare and give the schwag to the attendees.
  • Support the attendees while moving and searching for their talks.
  • Support the speakers.
  • And whatever happens.

On the other hand, there are also a lot of advantages:

  • Networking. Codemotion is a great opportunity to get to know a lot of incredible people.
  • Get first-hand experience about how the organization of an event for more than a thousand people feels like.
  • Get a T-shirt and special schwag as a Codemotion organizer.
  • You will be able to assist to some talks within reason. Remember, you came to help.
